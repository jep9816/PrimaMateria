Series

Index

A chemical series is a group of chemical elements whose physical and chemical characteristics vary progressively from one end of the series to another. The chemical series are as follows:

  • Actinides
  • Alkali Earth Metals
  • Alkali Metals
  • Halogens
  • Lanthanides
  • Metalloids (Metals)
  • Metalloids (Nonmetals)
  • Metals
  • Noble Gases
  • Nonmetals
  • Transactinides
  • Transition Metals
