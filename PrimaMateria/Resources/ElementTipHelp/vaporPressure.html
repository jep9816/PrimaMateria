Vapor Pressure

Index

The pressure exerted when a material in its solid or liquid state is in equilibrium with it's gas state. As this is changes as a function of temperature, vapor pressure must be qualified by the relevant temperature.

  • s - Indicated the substance is a solid at this temperature.
  • e - Indicated an extrapolation beyond the region where experimental measurements exist.
  • i - Indicated the value was calculated from ideal gas thermodynamic functions, sucah as those in the JANAF Thermodynamic tables.
